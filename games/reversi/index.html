Reversi

You: 2 AI: 2 Wins: 0

Your turn.

You are dark and move first · place a disc to outflank and flip the AI's pieces.

How to play

  • Goal: finish with more discs than the AI. You play dark and move first.
  • Legal move: you may only place a disc where it traps one or more of the AI's discs in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) between the disc you place and another of yours. Every trapped disc flips to your colour. Cells where you can move are marked with a dot.
  • Passing: if you have no legal move, your turn is skipped automatically. If neither side can move, the game ends.
  • Difficulty: Easy looks one move ahead (and sometimes plays randomly), Medium three, Hard four — and the AI values corners and edges, so they're worth fighting for.
  • Scoring: the board fills up (or both sides get stuck); whoever has more discs wins. Your total wins against the AI are saved on this device.
