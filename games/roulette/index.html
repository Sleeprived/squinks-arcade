How to play

  • Goal: grow your chip bankroll. You start with 1,000 chips; your highest-ever balance (Peak) is recorded.
  • Place bets: choose a chip value (5 / 25 / 100), then tap any bet to stack that many chips on it. You can spread chips across several bets in one spin. Chips aren't taken from your bankroll until you spin.
  • Bet types & payouts: Red/Black, Even/Odd, 1–18/19–36 pay 1:1; the dozens (1st/2nd/3rd 12) pay 2:1; a straight bet on a single number (0–36) pays 35:1.
  • Spin: the wheel lands on 0–36. 0 is green and loses every even-money and dozen bet — that's the house edge. Winning bets return your stake plus the payout.
  • Out of chips: if your bankroll drops below the minimum bet, a Rebuy button resets you to 1,000 (your Peak is kept). Reset clears the bankroll and Peak.
