How to play

  • Goal: blast every asteroid. Clearing the field starts the next, busier wave. Survive as long as you can.
  • Fly: the ↺ and ↻ buttons turn your ship; Thrust accelerates in the direction you face. There's no brake — momentum carries you, so thrust in short taps.
  • Thrust setting: the Thrust dropdown sets how punchy your engine is — Low, Normal, or High (each raises both acceleration and top speed). Your choice is saved on this device.
  • Shoot: tap Fire (or hold to fire repeatedly). On a keyboard use ←/→ to turn, ↑ to thrust, Space to fire.
  • Splitting: big rocks break into two medium rocks, mediums into two smalls, smalls into nothing. Smaller rocks move faster but are worth more.
  • Wrap-around: fly off one edge and you reappear on the opposite side — so do the asteroids and your bullets.
  • Lives: start with 3, lose one when a rock hits your ship (you respawn briefly invulnerable and blinking). Earn a bonus life every 10,000 points, up to 5. Game over at 0.
  • Pause: use the Pause button at the top (Space is reserved for firing).
