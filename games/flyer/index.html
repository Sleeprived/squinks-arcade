How to play

  • Goal: keep flying and pass through as many gaps as you can. Each gap you clear is a point.
  • Flap: tap anywhere on the board, or press Space, ↑, or W. Every flap gives a quick upward boost; gravity does the rest.
  • Don't crash: touching a pipe or the ground ends the run. The ceiling just stops you — you can't fly off the top.
  • It speeds up: the further you get, the faster the world scrolls and the tighter the gaps become — calm at first, frantic later.
  • Pause: use the Pause button at the top (Space is reserved for flapping).
  • Best score is saved on this device. Hit Reset to clear it.
