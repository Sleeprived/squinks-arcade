How to play

  • Goal: repeat ever-longer flashing sequences from memory. Each round the pattern adds one more flash.
  • Watch: press Start. The four panels light up one at a time in a sequence — memorise the order.
  • Repeat: when it's your turn, tap the panels in the same order. Tap a wrong panel and the round ends. (On a keyboard, keys 1 2 3 4 map to the panels.)
  • This Simon is visual-only — there are no sounds, so watch the lights closely. It also speeds up as you climb.
  • Scoring: your level is how many rounds you completed. Your best level is saved on this device.
