How to play

  • Goal: clear every brick by bouncing the ball off your paddle. Clear them all to advance to the next, faster level.
  • Move: drag your finger or mouse across the play area to slide the paddle. On a keyboard use ←/→ or A/D.
  • Launch: your ball rests on the paddle at the start of each life — tap the play area or press Space to launch. (Extra balls fan out and launch together.)
  • Aim: where the ball hits the paddle changes its angle. Hit it with the paddle's edge to send the ball sideways.
  • Speed: a ball speeds up with every brick it breaks, and each new level starts faster than the last — clearing a board gets more frantic the deeper you go.
  • Extra balls: you gain a ball at levels 2, 4, 8, and 16 (up to 5 in play at once). With several balls flying, you only lose a life when your last one falls.
  • Lives: you start with 3. Losing your last ball in play costs a life; the game ends at 0.
  • Scoring: higher brick rows are worth more points. Your best score is saved on this device.
  • Pause: the Pause button or Space (when the ball is in play) freezes the game.
