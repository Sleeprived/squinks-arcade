How to play

  • Goal: score as many hits as you can in 30 seconds.
  • Whack: moles pop out of the nine holes at random. Tap (or click) one while it's up to bop it for points; it ducks back instantly.
  • Speed up: as the clock winds down, moles appear more often and stay up for less time — so your reactions have to get quicker.
  • Scoring: every hit is worth 10 points. Missing a mole costs nothing but lost time. Your best score is saved on this device.
  • Replay: press Start (or Play again) to run a fresh 30-second round.
