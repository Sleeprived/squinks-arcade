How to play

  • Goal: slide the numbered tiles until they read 1 to 15 left-to-right, top-to-bottom, with the empty gap in the bottom-right corner.
  • Move: tap any tile that sits directly next to the empty gap and it slides into the gap. Only tiles touching the gap can move.
  • Keyboard: the arrow keys slide a tile into the gap from that direction.
  • Timer: the clock starts on your first move and stops the instant the puzzle is solved.
  • Records: your fewest moves and fastest time are saved separately on this device. New deals a fresh, always-solvable scramble.
